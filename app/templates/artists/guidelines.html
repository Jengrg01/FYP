Professional Makeup Artist Guidelines

Standards and Best Practices to be followed by Lumiere Artists

Professional Standards

  • Always arrive 15 minutes early to set up your station and prepare for clients.
  • Maintain a clean, organized kit with properly sanitized tools and products.
  • Dress professionally in all-black attire with hair pulled back and minimal personal jewelry.
  • Provide a detailed consultation before beginning any makeup application.
  • Be prepared with a diverse range of foundation shades to accommodate all skin tones.

Hygiene & Sanitation

Tools & Brushes

  • Clean brushes after each client using a professional brush cleaner.
  • Deep clean all brushes weekly with appropriate brush shampoo.
  • Replace sponges after each use or sanitize with 70% alcohol.

Product Handling

  • Always use disposable applicators for cream and liquid products.
  • Scrape products onto a sanitized palette instead of using directly from containers.
  • Spray powder products with 70% alcohol between clients.

Personal Hygiene

  • Wash hands thoroughly before and after each client.
  • Keep nails short and clean to prevent product contamination.
  • Use hand sanitizer frequently throughout applications.

Client Communication

  • Discuss client expectations and preferences before beginning application.
  • Explain your process and product choices throughout the service.
  • Address concerns immediately and make adjustments as needed.
  • Provide aftercare instructions and product recommendations.
  • Follow up with clients after special events to ensure satisfaction.

Kit Essentials

Base Products

  • Primers for different skin types
  • Foundation range (minimum 15 shades)
  • Concealers in light, medium, and deep tones
  • Setting powders (translucent and tinted)

Color Products

  • Neutral eyeshadow palette
  • Colorful eyeshadow palette
  • Blushes in cream and powder formulas
  • Bronzers for various skin tones
  • Highlighters (subtle and intense)

Tools

  • Complete brush set (minimum 15 brushes)
  • Beauty sponges
  • Disposable applicators
  • Lash curler and tweezers
  • Mixing palette and spatula
