About Lumiere

A platform connecting clients with professional makeup artists and providing personalized makeup style recommendations.

Lumiere is a modern beauty platform designed to simplify the process of finding and booking professional makeup artists while also helping users discover makeup styles that enhance their natural features.

We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful, and the right makeup can be a powerful tool for self-expression.

Our platform makes professional makeup services accessible to everyone, regardless of their experience with makeup or knowledge of beauty trends. Through innovative technology and a curated network of talented artists, we're transforming how people approach beauty.
